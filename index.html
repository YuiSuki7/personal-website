About

Not just a developer.
Not just a community manager.

I'm Komi (go by Lucyy online). By day, I manage CA & LATAM trading communities. By night, I build Discord bots, scrape social data, and explore digital sociology.

The unusual part? I combine skills most people keep separate:

  • CFD / Trading — Forex, indices, commodities markets
  • Python Automation — bots, scrapers, pipeline tools
  • Community Operations — multi-lingual (EN/ES/PT/中文) growth
  • Digital Sociology — Bourdieu, structural analysis of online communities

Projects

Things I've built and maintain

Discord · Python

CFD Trading Discord Bot

An all-in-one Discord bot for CFD communities — real-time alerts, community monitoring, and automated engagement tools. Built with discord.py, designed for multi-lingual audiences.

Telegram · Webhook

Ultima Radar Monitor

Keyword-based monitoring system for Telegram communities. Scans EN/ES/PT conversations for key topics and alerts Discord channels via webhook. Runs as a cron-scheduled pipeline.

Lark · Browser Automation

CM Reader Pipeline

Automated reading of Lark messages via CDP (Chrome DevTools Protocol), avoiding API approval processes. Feeds content into Discord for team coordination.

Research · Writing

Digital Sociology Research

Exploring how Bourdieu's Distinction theory maps onto online trading communities. Using scraped community data to analyze structural holes and social capital in CFD spaces.
